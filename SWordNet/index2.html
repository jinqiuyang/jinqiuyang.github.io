A Collection of Semantically Related Words from the Paper:

SWordNet: Inferring Semantically Related Words fromn Software Context

Introduction

We make publicly available manually verified semantically related words (referred to as rPairs) in order for other people to:

  • Check our results, in the spirit of any scientific work.
  • Provide a software version of WordNet and share with the research community.

We have chosen to publish the correct rPairs by manual verification in our MSR'12 paper.

Data

In each rPair file, Column 1&2 are a pair of words, Column 3 shows the one of the five category (synonym, antonym, near antonym, related, identifier, please refer to our MSR'12 paper for more details). Column 4&+ shows the context where this rPair is discovered.
